About me

I'm Chinmayee Kusnur, a user experience designer and a third-year Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) student. Since childhood, I have been highly observant about things around me and how they are portrayed, which is essential for a UX designer. I focus on getting the information communicated smoothly without any hindrance. I thrive on challenges and am passionate about designing products that make life easy. Therefore, I make minimalistic but functional design decisions and amalgamate grids, layouts, colour, and fonts while designing. My design process starts with brainstorming ideas via flowcharts on paper, making low-fidelity mockups on paper, and finally making high-fidelity mockups on Figma.

I am also very fond of about photography and I do take photos during my free time. I feel like the skill I use in photography really helps me when I design as the concepts like color and proportion are similar. Lastly, I love food. I try to explore different cuisines and one good meal will instantly make my day and lift my mood.
