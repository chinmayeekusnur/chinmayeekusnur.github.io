FORM Internship

Group photo from FORM Retreat, my favourite memory (May 2022)
About the internship
I had an amazing opportunity this summer to intern at FORM, as a digital product designer. I met some amazing people there who helped me grow as a designer and I will always be grateful for this chance I got.
My primary responsibilities were to work closely with other members of the Product team to brainstorm ideas to improve swim experience for users. Secondly, to craft UX deliverables such as annotated user flows and wireframes to effectively communicate design ideas. Lastly, to deliver high-fidelity UI designs for iOS, Android and FORM goggles.
My experience
I have learned everything I know about product design through this internship in terms of applying what I learned in school in a real company and working with legitimate users. However, the two main things I learned were how to communicate clearly, confidently and effectively & to use a design system on Figma properly.
As a designer, it was essential for me to learn the art of presenting designs well to the team and effectively conveying my idea. Unfortunately, it took some time for me to be organized and efficient in this matter. But now, I can present my designs confidently and say why a particular idea works to improve user experience. Secondly, I was exposed to a design system more extensive than I had ever used before. I learned how to use components, instances and variants, which helped me be more consistent and detail-oriented.
